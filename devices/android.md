# Android eSIM Compatibility

The Android ecosystem encompasses a wide range of manufacturers and models, and eSIM support varies widely by device and region. Google, Samsung, Huawei, and other brands have introduced eSIM in select models, but not all devices support it.

## Supported devices
- **Google Pixel**: Pixel 3 and newer models support eSIM; Pixel 7 and later can host multiple eSIM profiles.
- **Samsung Galaxy**: Recent Galaxy S20/S21/S22/S23 and Z Fold/Flip series include eSIM support on most international variants.
- **Other brands**: Some Huawei, Oppo, and Motorola devices offer eSIM, but availability depends on the market.
